  • About Us

    • What is Sudan Pathways?

    At Sudan Pathways, we are dedicated to being accurate, transparent, and innovative. Our main goal is to provide Sudanese people with clear and complete information to help them navigate the process of obtaining refugee status and securing a refugee visa. We use trusted sources such as the UNHCR (United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees), government databases, and reliable websites that share our mission. By doing so, we aim to help individuals find safe countries that can offer essential services and protection.

    Future Plans

    Sudan Pathways aims to expand our platform to include detailed information on all African countries that are welcoming to asylum-seekers, refugees, and internally displaced persons. In mid-November, we will relaunch our mobile app and introduce an artificial intelligence chatbot to our website to make accessing information easier and faster. This new feature will help you find the information you need more efficiently, supporting your journey towards safety and stability.
